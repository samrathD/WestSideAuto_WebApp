About Us

West Side Autoworks is your go-to destination for automotive excellence. We specialize in enhancing your driving experience with a range of services tailored to meet your vehicle's evolving needs. From seamless Carplay screen installations for modern connectivity to state-of-the-art backup camera installations ensuring safety and convenience, we've got you covered. Our expertise extends to headlight restorations, ambient lighting installations, and even starlight installations for those who want to bring a touch of the cosmos to their ride. At West Side Autoworks, we're not just about traditional automotive services; we're passionate about crafting an unparalleled driving experience for every enthusiast.
